LogParse.net Policy

We are committed to respecting your privacy. This policy describes and governs our collection and use of information. Before you use our site, please review this policy carefully.

As developers ourselves, we are well aware that log files can contain sensitive information. Therefore, you may be rightfully interested in the privacy policy of this site.

If you are using this site, you are most likely a developer capable of inspecting the network traffic produced by it. Feel free to inspect the network traffic to see that all information submitted stays entirely within your browser.

  • Your logs do not leave your browser
  • We do not keep or inspect any files or text submitted/uploaded/processed
  • We do not ask for or require any personal information to use this site

  • Measure ad performance. The performance and effectiveness of ads that you see or interact with can be measured.
